Passionate educators who care

Seamless learning from pre-school to post-school

We are a system of schools with a consistent learning approach that develops the skills students need now and in the future - communication, collaboration, creativity and critical thinking.

Individualised learning

Catholic schools promote education for all – celebrating the diversity and achievements of every student and encouraging them to discover their abilities and reach their full potential.

Learn more

Quality schooling that is affordable and inclusive

A quality Catholic education is affordable and accessible for every family! As a guide, the cost of a Catholic education is around $6-$8 a day for primary and $11-$18 a day a day for a child in Year 7*. Significant discounts apply for siblings. Ask about our flexible payment plans.

No child will be refused enrolment from a Catholic school because of a family's inability to pay school fees.
